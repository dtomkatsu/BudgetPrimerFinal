About LAW ENFORCEMENT

The Legislature authorized the creation of a new Department of Law Enforcement during the 2022 session through HB2171. Its purpose is to "consolidate and administer the criminal law enforcement and investigation functions," and now has responsibility for some programs that were previously administered by other departments. It currently manages Narcotics Enforcement, the Sheriff's Office, Criminal Investigation, Enforcement Training, and Homeland Security.
